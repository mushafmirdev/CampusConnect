Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I apply to Campus Connect?

You can apply through our online application form. The process includes submitting your application, participating in a virtual interview, and completing our selection process.

What are the eligibility requirements?

We welcome students from all universities worldwide who are passionate about leadership and making a positive impact. There are no specific GPA or major requirements.

Is there a cost to join?

Campus Connect is completely free for students. We believe that leadership development should be accessible to everyone, regardless of financial background.

How long is the program?

Our comprehensive program runs for 12 months, with different phases focusing on foundation building, skill development, practical application, and mastery.
