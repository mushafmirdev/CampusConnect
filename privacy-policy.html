Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: January 2025

Agreement to Terms

By accessing or using Campus Connect's services, you agree to be bound by these Privacy Policy and all applicable laws and regulations. If you do not agree with any of these terms, you are prohibited from using or accessing this site.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, apply for our programs, participate in events, or contact us for support.

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • University affiliation and academic information
  • Profile information and interests
  • Application materials and responses
  • Communication preferences

Usage Information

  • Website usage and interaction data
  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and location data
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, communicate with you, and personalize your experience.

Service Delivery

  • Process applications and manage program participation
  • Provide mentorship and networking opportunities
  • Organize and manage events and workshops
  • Send important updates and notifications

Communication

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Send newsletters and program updates
  • Share relevant opportunities and resources
  • Conduct surveys and gather feedback

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

With Your Consent

We may share your information with your explicit consent, such as when you opt into networking opportunities or mentorship programs.

Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform, conducting our business, or serving our users.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can also opt out of certain communications and data processing activities.

Access and Control

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage patterns, and provide personalized content and advertisements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Notification Methods

When we make significant changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you through one or more of the following methods:

  • Email notification to the address associated with your account
  • Prominent notice on our website homepage
  • In-app notification if you have our mobile application
  • Direct communication through our platform messaging system

What Constitutes a Significant Change

We consider the following types of changes to be significant and will provide advance notice:

  • Changes to how we collect, use, or share your personal information
  • New purposes for processing your data
  • Changes to your rights and choices regarding your information
  • Updates to our data retention practices
  • Changes to our contact information or how to reach us

Your Continued Use

Your continued use of our services after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. If you do not agree with the changes, you may discontinue using our services and request deletion of your account.

Policy Version History

We maintain a record of significant changes to this Privacy Policy. You can request access to previous versions of this policy by contacting us at privacy@campusconnect.org.
